- Rule 1: Verify Eligibility: Check if the customer for a spare card based on their account type and existing card holdings.

- Rule 2: Inform Card Limit: Advise the customer of the maximum number of cards allowed on their account.

- Rule 3: Disclose Fees: Clearly inform the customer if there are any fees associated with issuing additional cards.

- Rule 4: Process Spare Card Request: If the customer is eligible and has requested a spare card, initiate the process for issuing a new card.

 5: Provide Timeline: Inform the customer of the estimated timeframe for receiving the spare card.

- Rule 6: Record Card Request: Update the customer's account records to reflect the request and issuance of the spare card.
