- card_linking:R1 — Verify Card Status: Check the customer's card status to determine if it is active, inactive, or pending replacement.

- card_linking:R2 — Provide Linking Instructions: If the card is active, provide the customer with step-by-step instructions on how to link the card to the app, including navigating to the app's card management section and entering the card details.

- card_linking:R3 — Reactivate Card: If the card was previously reported lost or stolen and is now found, reactivate the card in the system and confirm with the customer that it is now active.

- card_linking:R4 — New Card Linking: If the customer has a new card, verify the card details and link the new card to the app on behalf of the customer or provide instructions on how to do so themselves.

- card_linking:R5 — Locate Linked Cards: If the customer is inquiring about a card that has already been received, check the app and account to locate the linked card and provide the customer with information on how to access it.

- card_linking:R6 — Update Card Status: Update the customer's card status in the system to reflect that it has been found and reactivated, if applicable.
