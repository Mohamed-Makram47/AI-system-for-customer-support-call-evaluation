- Rule 1: Provide Transfer Timing Framework: The agent must provide the customer with a clear explanation of the transfer timing framework, which includes: 
    - Same-day or next-day transfers for domestic US transactions
    - 2-5 business days for non-domestic US transactions (e.g., Europe, China)
    - Cut-off times for same-day processing (e.g., 4 pm EST)

- Rule 2: Quote Specific Transfer Times: The agent must quote specific transfer times based on the origin and destination of the transfer, 
    - US domestic: same-day or 1 business day
    - Europe: 2-3 business days
    - China: 3-5 business days

- Rule 3: Identify Expedited Transfer Options: If the customer requests faster transfer options, the agent must identify and explain available expedited transfer services, 
    - e.g., same-day or next-day transfers for an additional fee

- Rule 4: Provide Cut-off Times: The agent must inform the customer of the cut-off times for same-day processing and how it affects their transfer, 
    - e.g., transfers initiated after 4 pm EST will be processed the next business day

- Rule 5: Clarify Weekend and Holiday Processing: The agent must clarify how weekends and holidays affect transfer processing times, 
    - e.g., transfers initiated on a Friday may not be processed until Monday

- Rule 6: Offer Tracking Information: If the transfer is still pending, the agent must offer to provide tracking information or a reference number to help the customer track the status of their transfer.
