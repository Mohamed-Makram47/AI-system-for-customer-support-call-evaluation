**Verifying Identity Policy Manual for Call Center Agents**

When interacting with customers, it is essential to verify their identity to ensure the security and integrity of their accounts and our banking institution. The following rules must be followed:

- **Rule 1: Verify Identity for All Sensitive Transactions**: Agents must verify the identity of customers for all sensitive transactions, including but not limited to, withdrawals over $10,000, changes to account beneficiary information, and requests for account closures. Verification must occur before processing the transaction.

- **Rule 2: Use the Three-Factor Authentication Method**: To verify a customer's identity, agents must use at least two of the following three factors: (1) something the customer knows (e.g., account number, date of birth, or social security number), (2) something the customer has (e.g., a one-time password sent to their mobile device or a token), or (3) something the customer is (e.g., voice recognition or biometric data). For high-risk transactions, agents must use all three factors.

- **Rule 3: Confirm Customer Information through Independent Sources**: When verifying a customer's identity, agents must confirm their information through independent sources, such as our internal database or external verification services. Agents must not rely solely on information provided by the customer.

- **Rule 4: Document Verification Attempts and Outcomes**: Agents must document all verification attempts, including successful and unsuccessful attempts, in the customer's account records. The documentation must include the date, time, and method of verification, as well as the outcome.

- **Rule 5: Escalate Unverified or Suspicious Transactions**: If an agent is unable to verify a customer's identity or suspects that the customer may be attempting to commit fraud, they must escalate the issue to their supervisor or the fraud prevention team immediately. The agent must not process any transactions or provide sensitive information until the issue is resolved.
