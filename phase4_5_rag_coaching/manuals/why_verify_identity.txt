- why_verify_identity:R3 — Offer Alternative Verification Methods: If a customer expresses concern or refusal to use a specific method for verification (e.g., voice biometrics, answering security questions), the agent must offer at least two alternative methods for verification that are available and compliant with internal policies.

- why_verify_identity:R6 — Refusal Protocol: If a customer refuses to verify their identity, the agent must inform them of the potential consequences, such as limited account access or account closure, as per the bank's policy.

- why_verify_identity:R7 — Confirm Verification Completion: Once verification is completed, the agent must confirm with the customer that their identity has been successfully verified and that they now have full access to their account and its features.
