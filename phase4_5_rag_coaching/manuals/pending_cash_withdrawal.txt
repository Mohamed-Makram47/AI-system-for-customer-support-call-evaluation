- Rule 1: Verify Transaction Status: Check the customer's account to confirm if the cash withdrawal transaction is still pending and verify the timestamp of the transaction.

- Rule 2: Cancel Pending Transaction: If the customer's card was declined or the transaction was not completed, immediately cancel the pending cash withdrawal transaction.

- Rule 3: Provide Transaction Timeline: Inform the customer of the expected timeline for the transaction to be updated in their account, which is typically 2-3 business days, but may vary depending on the bank's policies.

- Rule 4: Investigate Declined Transactions: If the customer's card was declined at an ATM, investigate the transaction to ensure that the customer was not charged for the transaction and confirm that the transaction was properly declined.

- Rule 5: Update Customer on Pending Transaction: If the cash withdrawal is still pending, inform the customer that the transaction will be updated in their account once it is processed or cancelled, and provide a clear explanation of the next steps.

- Rule 6: Resolve Pending Transaction: If the pending cash withdrawal transaction exceeds the standard processing time (typically 2-3 business days), proactively investigate and resolve the issue by either cancelling the transaction or updating the account status.
