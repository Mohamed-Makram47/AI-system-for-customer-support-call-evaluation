**Receiving Money Policy Manual for Call Center Agents**

When handling customer inquiries and transactions related to receiving money, agents must follow these guidelines to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and company policies:

- **Verify Customer Identity**: Before processing any transaction or providing information related to receiving money, agents must verify the customer's identity by asking for and confirming their name, date of birth, and account information (if applicable).

- **Disclose Fees and Terms**: Agents must clearly disclose any fees associated with receiving money, including but not limited to transfer fees, processing fees, and any other charges. Additionally, agents must explain the terms and conditions of the transaction, including the exchange rate (if applicable) and the estimated processing time.

- **Ensure Compliance with Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Regulations**: Agents must ensure that all transactions related to receiving money comply with AML regulations. This includes but is not limited to verifying the customer's identity, checking for suspicious activity, and reporting any transactions that exceed the reporting threshold.

- **Obtain Necessary Documentation**: Agents must obtain and review all necessary documentation related to the transaction, including but not limited to identification documents, proof of income, and documentation related to the source of funds.

- **Use Approved Scripts and Disclosures**: Agents must use approved scripts and disclosures when communicating with customers about receiving money. This includes but is not limited to scripts related to fee disclosures, terms and conditions, and AML regulations. Agents must also provide customers with a clear and concise summary of the transaction details and any next steps.
