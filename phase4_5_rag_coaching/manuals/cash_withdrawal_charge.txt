- Rule 1: Verify Fee Type: Confirm if the customer was charged a cash withdrawal fee by checking the transaction details and specifying the type of fee.

- Rule 2: Check Fee Waiver Eligibility: Inform the customer if they are eligible for fee waivers based on their account type or package.

- Rule 3: Explain Fee Structure: Clearly explain the cash withdrawal fee structure, including any applicable limits or conditions.

- Rule 4: Identify ATM Type: Determine if the customer used an out-of-network ATM and explain the associated fees.

- Rule 5: Review Account Terms: Review the customer's account terms and conditions to confirm if cash withdrawals are subject to fees.

- Rule 6: Provide Transaction Details: Provide the customer with detailed information about the transaction, including the date, time, location, and amount of the withdrawal.

- Rule 7: Offer Alternative Solutions: Offer alternative solutions or account options that may reduce or eliminate cash withdrawal fees.
