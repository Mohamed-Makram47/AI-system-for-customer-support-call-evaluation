- request_refund:R5 — Explain Refund Timeline: Explain the estimated timeline for processing the refund, including any relevant deadlines or expectations for receiving the refund.

- request_refund:R6 — Offer Cancellation or Reversal: If the customer requests a refund for a recent transaction, offer to cancel or reverse the transaction if possible, and provide a confirmation of the cancellation or reversal.
