**Edit Personal Details Policy Manual for Call Center Agents**

When handling customer requests to edit personal details, agents must follow these rules:

- **Verify Customer Identity**: Before making any changes to a customer's personal details, agents must verify the customer's identity using their name, date of birth, and either their account number or social security number to ensure they are speaking with the account holder.

- **Use Approved Scripts and Templates**: Agents must use approved scripts and templates provided by the bank for editing personal details to ensure consistency and compliance with regulatory requirements.

- **Obtain Explicit Consent**: For changes involving sensitive information, such as address or phone number updates, agents must obtain explicit consent from the customer to proceed with the edit, and document the customer's consent in the customer's account notes.

- **Update Only Authorized Fields**: Agents are only authorized to update personal details that are within their designated scope of authority, as outlined in their job description and training materials; any requests to update other fields must be escalated to a supervisor or specialist.

- **Document All Changes**: All changes made to a customer's personal details must be thoroughly documented in the customer's account notes, including the date, time, and description of the change, as well as the customer's consent (if applicable), to provide a clear audit trail.
