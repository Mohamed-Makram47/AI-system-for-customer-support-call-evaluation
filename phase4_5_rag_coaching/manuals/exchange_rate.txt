- Rule 1: Provide Exchange Rate Source: Inform the customer that exchange rates are sourced from a reputable third-party provider, such as Bloomberg or Reuters, and provide the specific provider's name.

- Rule 2: Explain Exchange Rate Calculation: Clearly explain to the customer that exchange rates for transfers are calculated based on the current market rate at the time of the transfer, and may include a markup or margin.

- Rule 3: Disclose Exchange Rate Policy: Provide the customer with a detailed explanation of the bank's exchange rate policy, including any applicable fees, markups, or margins.

- Rule 4: Quote Specific Exchange Rate: Provide the customer with the specific exchange rate being applied to their transaction, including the rate and any associated fees.

- Rule 5: Describe Exchange Rate Basis: Explain to the customer the basis for determining the exchange rate, such as the interbank rate or a specific currency benchmark.

- Rule 6: Provide Context on App Exchange Rates: Inform the customer that exchange rates on the app are indicative and may vary depending on the time of day, market conditions, and other factors.

- Rule 7: Refer to Published Rates: Direct the customer to where they can find published exchange rates on the bank's website or mobile app, if applicable.
