- order_physical_card:R3 — Provide Card Delivery Information: The agent must inform the customer about the card delivery process, including estimated delivery times, delivery locations (e.g., home, branch), and any associated delivery fees.

- order_physical_card:R4 — Guide Customer Through Ordering Process: The agent must walk the customer through the steps to order a physical card, including providing the necessary information (e.g., address, identification) and any required documentation.

- order_physical_card:R5 — Confirm Card Details: The agent must confirm the customer's understanding of the physical card details, including the card type, delivery address, and any associated fees.

- order_physical_card:R6 — Provide Tracking Information: If applicable, the agent must provide the customer with tracking information for the physical card shipment.
