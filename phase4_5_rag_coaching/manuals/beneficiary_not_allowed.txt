- Rule 1: Verify Beneficiary Status: Check if the beneficiary is eligible to receive transfers and confirm if they are an approved payee.

- Rule 2: Check Transfer Limits: Verify if the customer has exceeded their daily/ monthly transfer limits to the beneficiary.

- Rule 3: Identify Beneficiary Restrictions: Determine if there are any restrictions on the beneficiary account that prevent transfers.

- Rule 4: Validate Beneficiary Information: Confirm if the beneficiary information provided by the customer is accurate and matches the records.

- Rule 5: Provide Reason for Transfer Failure: Inform the customer of the specific reason for the transfer failure, such as "Beneficiary not approved" or "Transfer limit exceeded".

- Rule 6: Offer Alternative Solutions: Suggest alternative solutions to the customer, such as adding the beneficiary as an approved payee or waiting for the transfer limit to reset.
